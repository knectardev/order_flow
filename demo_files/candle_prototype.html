Asymmetric Candle Prototype

CVD-weighted body shading + liquidity-weighted wick tips

Encoding
Green/red body: close > open (up) or < open (down)
Top half shade: darker = aggressive participation reinforcing the candle's direction at upper prices
Bottom half shade: same logic at lower prices — darker = stronger conviction
Filled circle: rejection by absorption — heavy volume hit a wall of opposing liquidity (committed defender, level likely holds)
Hollow circle: rejection by exhaustion — aggressive flow faded, no wall (weak level, often fails on retest)
Circle diameter: strength of the rejection. At most one circle per candle.
Wick position: low first = upper wick on right edge, lower wick on left edge; high first = opposite
Body width: bid-ask spread (narrower = 1 tick, wider = 2+ ticks)
