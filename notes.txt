A persistent tooltip you can dismiss with click or escape lets you read while looking. The bar-hover data tooltip should stay hover-only since you're scanning many bars.

2. **Use the (i) info icon, not just legend hover, as the entry point.** That icon I see at the top is sitting unused for what looks like its native purpose.
